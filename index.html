We are a fun, friendly, mixed softball club, playing in 2019 in the Greater London Softball Mixed League Majors Division 2 (The Mob) and Minors Division 5 (The Mafia). As a result of our expansion to running two teams, we are looking to recruit players for the new season!

The outdoor league season runs from April/May to August on weeknights, with various tournaments at weekends between April and September. We tend to practice every Sunday on Wandsworth Common (John Archer Way) during the league season. We are also competing in the London Indoor Softball League during the winter.

Fees for the season are £75 and these include all league matches and training. If joining halfway through the season it is £40, and later than that would be £5 per game. Tournaments have a separate cost of £15 per day.

We would welcome new players regardless of experience.

Please feel free to get in contact via email if you are interested in playing for us.
